What is lovedrop
Lovedrop can airdrop erc20 tokens directly to the nfts ids. Instead of an address based snapshot.
There is no snapshot i love you. There is no snapshot, users can still buy nfts, payback blur loans and claim tokens if that nft still has them.
Because its tied to specific ids, you can drop to specific attributes or list of ids.

Notice
Lovedrop is still in early development. Creating drops is at your own risk!
Lovedrop only fully supports the collections in the selector at the create page. Most erc712 10k pfp collections with their base URI on ipfs, will likely work but be carefull and dm me on twitter if anything looks off!

Not support yet: anything other than erc712, collections still minting (ex crypto kitties), non standard meta data (ex og cryptopunks), with centralized metadata that has restrictive CORS (ex azuki)
